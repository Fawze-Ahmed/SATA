# SATA Website

Luxury bilingual static website for SATA, positioned as a Dubai sovereign operations room.

## Included

- Arabic and English language switching with RTL/LTR support.
- Luxury modern-classic visual style with gold, emerald, dark executive tones, and tech city motion.
- Five dedicated service paths:
  - VIP Concierge & Logistics.
  - Corporate Setup & Legal Support.
  - Real Estate & Headquarters.
  - Software, AI & Digital Transformation.
  - Fintech, Smart Trading & Cybersecurity.
- Dedicated service detail pages through `service.html?id=0..4&lang=ar|en`.
- Investor Suite section for AI trading and cybersecurity.
- Contact form matching the requested investor fields.
- Smart chatbot with common investor questions and consultation-focused answers.
- Floating WhatsApp button linked to `971522581055`.
- SVG logo and brand mark in `assets/`.

Open `index.html` in a browser to preview the website.
